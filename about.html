About Pawlinju

Pawlinju was formerly a family business founded by Paulene Benaid in 2020, at the height of the pandemic. With the help of her family, her business thrived and prospered.

Pawlinju is a pastry-based business that aims to offer quality and affordable dessert.

Our Journey

Pawlinju was born out of a love for baking and a desire to share our scrumptious crinkle cookies with the world. With a commitment to crafting each batch with care, our journey began in a cozy kitchen, and our flavors have been delighting taste buds ever since.

Our Mission

Our mission at Pawlinju is simple yet profound – to bring joy to your day through the magic of our homemade crinkles. We believe that life's sweet moments should be celebrated and cherished, and what better way to do so than with a perfectly crinkled cookie?

Join Us

Whether you're looking to satisfy a sweet craving, create a memorable gift, or simply savor the delight of a crinkle cookie, Pawlinju is here for you. Join us in the joy of homemade crinkles, and let our cookies be a part of your story.

Thank you for being a part of the Pawlinju family, and we can't wait to share more sweet moments with you.
